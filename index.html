Beatzz is a minimalistic and easy-to-use music streaming application like Spotify/YouTube Music, but free, and no ads forever.

Download
for Android 5+

Intuitive UI

Beatzz has a minimalistic, unique and intuitive user interface, which makes it easy to navigate and pleasing to the eyes.

It also uses a dark overall color scheme, so that you now have a night-ready slick design.

Thoughtful UX

Beatzz cares about user experience so we have categorised every element of the app, allowing you to quickly find what you need.

Hence; Songs, Albums and Playlists are kept seperately to ensure a uncluttered experience, while also allowing for a sleek look.

Massive Library

Songs are obtained from the massive YouTube Music song library, with an option to download Youtube videos as audio.

You can search your songs by name of the song, artist, album. You can also play pre-made playlists for every mood or genre.

Custom Playlists

Not enough? We always love to jam to a playlist. Speaking of playlists, that's another feature Beatzz entails.

You can create an infinite number of easily shareable custom playlists from your song library with the click of a button.

Customisable

We value your choices and your opinions. This is the reason why Beatzz provides you the option to tune it in the way that best matches the uniqueness you possess.

Beatzz allows for three modes for songs - Download (which downloads songs for future offline sessions), Stream (Streams the song online), or 'Both' (Streams the song while also downloading it, so you don't have to wait for the download to finish).

Free forever!

Beatzz was, is, and will always remain free of charge, ad-free, and . The only source of monetary income from this app is (optional) donations.

The goal of Beatzz is not to earn money, but to promote creation of free applications that can stand up to proprietary and paid applications by huge organisations. Oh, and also to give everyone the best music experience possible. The application is still in Beta, but we accept all suggestions, and provide support for issues on Telegram. Thanks for giving us a go!
